Kitten Collector

How to Play:

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game, it is your Goal Value.
  • There are four kittens below, by clicking on a kitten you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to your Goal Value, and you lose if your total score goes over the Goal Value.
  • The value of each kitten is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal
